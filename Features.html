FEATURES

GetPaid is an end-to-end payment processing platform powering businesses of all sizes.
We take care of the payments infrastructure so you can focus on growing your business.
You can enjoy all the capabilities of GetPaid the second you sign up.

Accept Mobile Money

Accept payments via mobile money on your website, app or software. Easy setup and a single contract means you can start accepting payments in minutes. 

Accept Card Payments

Accept  payments from VISA, MASTERCARD, AMERICAN EXPRESS, DISCOVER, JCB and more credit and debit cards. Card payments connect your business with the rest of the world so you can grow globally.


Disbursement

Pay suppliers, employees or partners in an instant. Send money to multiple mobile numbers, paybill accounts, buy goods numbers or directly to bank accounts. 

Reports Dashboard

Understand your cash flows at a glance with an automated intelligent report dashboard. Know your customers with detailed metrics and keep records of payments made.

Multi-Currency Support

Accept payments in over 100 currencies as you grow your global enterprise.
Receive funds in KSh in moments at the best rates.

No Transaction Limits

Be confident as you can process up to 10 million KES per day easily. Transfer funds to or from bank accounts with a click. 

Business to Business Payments

Paying suppliers has never been this easy. Transfer funds directly into a Paybill Account Number or a bank account in minutes. 

Automate Payments

We've integrated with Odoo ERP to make it easy for you to automate payments.
