Frequently Asked Questions

What is GetPaid?

GetPaid is a payment solution allowing online Kenyan businesses to accept and access local and global payments easily, quickly and safely.

Which payment methods can I accept using GetPaid?

You can accept payments via PayPal,VISA and MASTERCARD credit and debit cards through Stripe and M-Pesa.

Can I use GetPaid on my own domain?

Yes, you can use GetPaid on your domain like www.mydomain.co.ke

How can I access payments made to my GetPaid account?

Simply login to your account, choose how you'd like to withdraw the money (either M-Pesa or bank account) and you'll have it instantly.

Do I need to have my own PayPal account?

Yes you do. If you need some help setting one up, send us an e-mail and we'll be glad to help.

Which currencies can I accept using GetPaid?

You can accept over 100 currencies.Soon, you'll be able to accept BitCoin and AliPay.
